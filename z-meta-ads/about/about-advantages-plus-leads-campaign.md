# About Advantage+ Leads Campaigns

> **Note:** This product or feature may not be available to you yet.

## Overview

Advantage+ leads campaigns is designed to maximize the performance of your leads
campaigns with less set up time and greater efficiency. Using AI, Advantage+
leads campaigns can help you generate qualified leads by delivering your ads to
more relevant audiences on more effective ad placements.

## Benefits of Using Advantage+ Leads Campaigns

### 1. Boost Performance with AI

Advantage+ leads campaigns uses AI to help you maximize performance and find the
most efficient opportunities to generate leads.

**Default AI-Powered Features:**

- Advantage+ campaign budget
- Advantage+ audience
- Advantage+ placements

**Performance Results:**

In early testing, leads campaigns that had Advantage+ on delivered:

- **14% lower** cost per lead (on average)
- **10% lower** cost per qualified lead (on average)

Compared to lead campaigns created with Advantage+ off.

> **Note:** You can adjust these settings at any time, if needed.

### 2. Streamlined Experience

In comparison to lead ads created using the manual campaign setup, Advantage+
leads campaigns requires fewer inputs from you during ad creation.

**Key Advantages:**

- Removes the need to run many campaigns with varying targeting and ad creatives
- Gives the ad delivery system more opportunities to reach people most likely to
  take action

### 3. Flexible Adjustments to Your Settings

You can access optional audience suggestions and controls to adjust the ads to
your specific business needs.

**Manual Control Options:**

- Manually select your placements (turns Advantage+ off)
- Set ad set budgets (turns Advantage+ off)

### 4. Access to Existing Lead Ad Features

You can still use all of the same options and ad features available in manual
leads campaigns.

**Available Conversion Locations:**

- Lead ads with instant form
- Website form
- Calling
- Lead ads that click to message

**Performance Goals:**

You can still use all of the same performance goals to optimize your lead ads
to:

- Maximize the number of leads
- Maximize conversion leads
