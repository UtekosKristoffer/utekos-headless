# About Leads Center in Meta Business Suite

Leads Center in Meta Business Suite helps you track, manage and nurture leads
who may be interested in your products or services. You can see your leads from:

- Organic business messaging
- Ads that click to message
- Lead ads

## Accessing Leads Center

To access the Leads Center:

1. Go to Meta Business Suite
2. Click **Leads Center** in the left menu, or
3. Click **All tools** and find it under **Engage audience**

## Viewing Your Leads

In Leads Center, you can choose between two visualization modes:

### Pipeline View

- Drag and drop leads into columns (e.g., Qualified, Converted)
- Track leads as they move across different stages

### Table View

- Organizes leads in rows
- View all relevant lead information
- Sort leads by:
  - Date added
  - Name
  - Source
  - Channel
  - Stage
  - Assigned owners

## Benefits of Using Leads Center in Meta Business Suite

In Leads Center, you can select a lead to view and edit their contact
information and use the following features:

### Track and Manage Leads' Progress

- By default, every lead is categorized as **Intake**
- Select a lead and click the **Stage** dropdown to categorize them
- Create custom stages to match your sales funnel
- Use default stages:
  - Intake
  - Qualified
  - Converted
  - Lost
  - Not qualified

### Message Your Leads

- Continue conversations with leads generated organically and from ads that
  click to message
- Available for both Messenger and Instagram
- Click the channel label (Instagram or Messenger) on the lead
- Chat head will surface at the bottom

### View and Download Answers

- If the lead was from an instant form, ads that click to message, or lead ads
  that click to message
- A **Complete form** label indicates viewable form answers
- Access answers from the lead's contact card

### Assign an Owner to Your Leads

- Select a lead and click the **Unassigned** dropdown
- Assign the lead to a specific person with Leads Center access
- Useful for teams with multiple people following up

### Filter and Sort Your Leads

**Filter by:**

- Forms
- Date added
- Source
- Assigned owners
- Labels you've created

**Sort by:**

- Date added
- Name
- Source
- Channel
- Stage
- Assigned owners

Use the **Add filter** and search bar at the top for additional options (e.g.,
Campaign ID, Ad ID).

### Schedule a Reminder

- Select a lead and click **Schedule a reminder**
- Set up a reminder for the assigned owner to follow up
- Allows more time without forgetting to reengage

### Add Labels and Notes to Your Leads

- Describe and organize leads with labels and notes
- Select a lead and click:
  - **Add label** insertion bar to create labels
  - **Write a note** insertion bar to include a note

### View Source Details

If you generated the lead from Meta ads, click the **Paid** source label to see
more details:

- **Ads that click to message**: View performance, audience and ad preview from
  a **View results** pop-up window
- **Instant form**: See a preview of the instant form they filled in

### Upload Leads

- Click the **+ Add leads** button in the top right corner
- Choose from dropdown menu:
  - **Create lead manually**
  - **Upload leads** to transfer multiple leads from an existing spreadsheet
- Centralize leads from Meta technologies and other sources in one place

### Export Leads

- Click the **Download** icon in the top right corner
- Download leads as a CSV file based on your current filter

> **Note:** You can also go to **All tools** and click **Instant forms** within
> Meta Business Suite or go to **Meta Ads Manager** to download your leads data.
