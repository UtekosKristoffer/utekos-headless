# Enable Lead Nurturing in Meta Ads Manager

> **Note**: This product or feature may not be available to you yet.

## Overview

The lead nurturing section in the Ads Manager can help you reach leads where
they're most active with tailored post-submission follow-ups through Meta's
messaging channels. Starting custom follow-up conversations can help strengthen
your connections and build trust with leads right after they submit your instant
form. Learn more about the benefits of using follow-up channels.

## Method 1: Enable Lead Nurturing in Ads Manager

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to Ads Manager**
   - Go to Ads Manager and select **Leads** as your ad objective
   - Click **Continue**
   - After you select an objective, you may see a campaign score on the right
     side of the page. Campaign score predicts how optimized your campaign is
     before publishing

2. **Configure Conversion Settings**
   - Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Conversion** section
     at the ad set level
   - Select **Instant forms** as your conversion location

3. **Set Up Instant Form**
   - Complete the ad creation steps until you reach the **Destination** section
     at the ad level
   - Select an existing instant form or complete instant form creation steps to
     create an instant form
   - Click **Create form**

4. **Configure Follow-up Channels**
   - In the **Lead nurturing** section, go to **Follow-up channels**
   - Check the box next to the channels you want to use:
     - Chat on Messenger
     - Chat on Instagram
     - Chat on WhatsApp
     - Instant form additional action(s)

   > **Note**: If your Page is not linked to a WhatsApp account, click **Connect
   > account** to connect your WhatsApp Business account. If your Page is linked
   > to a personal WhatsApp account, please go back to the Identity section and
   > select a Page that is connected to a WhatsApp Business account, or connect
   > a business account to your current Page.

5. **Configure Chat Builder**

   Under **Chat builder**, you can choose one of the following options:
   - **Built-in automation**:
     - Click **Edit**
     - Customize the greeting text, call to action, link and frequently asked
       questions
     - Preview how your chat may appear
     - Click **Save**

     > **Note**: The available customizations depend on the follow-up channels
     > you selected.

   - **Partner template**:
     - Click **Select flow**
     - Use the dropdown menus to choose your partner app and the template you
       want to use
     - Preview how your chat may appear
     - Click **Save**

6. **Publish Your Ad**
   - Complete the rest of your lead ad
   - Click **Publish** when you're ready

## Method 2: Enable Lead Nurturing from the Instant Form Editor

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to Ads Manager**
   - Go to Ads Manager and select **Leads** as your ad objective
   - Click **Continue**

2. **Configure Instant Form Ending**
   - Complete instant form creation steps until you reach the **Ending** section
   - In the **Ending** section, enter a headline and (optional) description

3. **Set Up Additional Actions**

   Under **Additional action**, select one of the following:
   - **Chat on WhatsApp**: To let people message you on WhatsApp

     > **Note**: If your Page is not linked to a WhatsApp account, click
     > **Connect account** to connect your WhatsApp Business account. If your
     > Page is linked to a personal WhatsApp account, please go back to the
     > Identity section and select a Page that is connected to a WhatsApp
     > Business account, or connect a business account to your current Page.
     - (Optional) Under **Call to action**, you can edit the default call to
       action. The call to action can be up to 40 characters.

4. **Connect with Leads in Messenger**

   Under **Connect with leads in Messenger**, you can:
   - Check the box next to **Start a conversation on Messenger**
   - Under **Select a partner app**, click **Select flow**
   - Use the dropdown menus to choose your partner app and the template you want
     to use
   - Preview how your chat may appear
   - Click **Save**

5. **Complete and Publish**
   - Click **Create form**
   - Complete the rest of your lead ad
   - Click **Publish** when you're ready
