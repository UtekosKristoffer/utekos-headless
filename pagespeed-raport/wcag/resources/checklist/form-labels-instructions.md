# Form Inputs, Labels, and Instructions

This document summarizes accessibility guidance for form labels, instructions,
input purpose, required fields, authentication, and related form behavior.

## Time Limits

| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
