# Animation, Motion, and Timed Content

This document summarizes accessibility guidance for animation, motion, autoplay,
flashing content, parallax effects, and timed interactions.

| Topic                  | Technique                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Testing Methodology                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Requirement Level | WCAG Criterion                                                                             |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Audio and Video        | See the [audio and video requirements](https://dequeuniversity.com/checklists/web/audio-video).                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | N/A                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Required          | Multiple                                                                                   |
| Flashing               | [Flashing](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/multimedia2/seizure-inducing-flashes/flashing-content): A page MUST NOT contain content that flashes more than 3 times per second unless that flashing content is sufficiently small, the flashes are of low contrast, and do not violate general flash thresholds. | [Testing methodology 2.3.1.a](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/a/2.3.1/2.3.1.a)                                                                                                                | Required          | [WCAG 2.3.1](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/three-flashes-or-below-threshold) |
| Parallax Effects       | [Keyboard-Accessible](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/multimedia2/animations-motion/parallax-effects#keyboard-accessible): All content and features within parallax scrolling content MUST be accessible by keyboard.                                                                                          | [Testing methodology 2.1.1.a](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/a/2.1.1/2.1.1.a)                                                                                                                | Required          | [WCAG 2.1.1](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/keyboard)                         |
| Parallax Effects       | [Text Color Contrast](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/multimedia2/animations-motion/parallax-effects#text-contrast): The contrast of the text against all parts of a moving background MUST be a minimum of 4.5 to 1 for small text or 3 to 1 for large or bold text.                                          | [Testing methodology 1.4.3.a](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/aa/1.4.3/1.4.3.a); [Testing methodology 1.4.3.b](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/aa/1.4.3/1.4.3.b) | Required          | [WCAG 1.4.3](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/contrast-minimum)                 |
| Parallax Effects       | [UI Component Color Contrast](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/visual-design2/contrast/ui-components#boundaries): The contrast of UI control boundaries compared to adjacent areas SHOULD be 3 to 1 to distinguish the UI control from adjacent areas.                                                          | [Testing methodology 1.4.11.b](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/aa/wcag21aa/1.4.11/1.4.11.b)                                                                                                   | Required          | [WCAG 1.4.11](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/non-text-contrast) (WCAG 2.1)    |
| User Control of Timing | [Autoplay](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/multimedia2/auto-play): A method MUST be provided to pause, stop, or hide any media content that begins playing automatically and lasts 5 seconds or more.                                                                                                          | N/A                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Required          | [WCAG 2.2.2](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/pause-stop-hide)                  |
| User Control of Timing | Time Limits: A method MUST be provided to allow users to control time limits by turning them off, adjusting them, or extending them, unless the timing is essential or the time limit is longer than 20 hours.                                                                                                   | [Testing methodology 2.2.1.a](https://dequeuniversity.com/class/dequeway/interpreting/wcag/a/2.2.1/2.2.1.a)                                                                                                                | Required          | [WCAG 2.2.1](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/timing-adjustable)                |
| User Control of Timing | Replay Option: A method SHOULD be provided to allow users to replay timed content that is finished or expired, unless replaying the content fundamentally alters its purpose or meaning.                                                                                                                         | N/A                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Best Practice     | N/A                                                                                        |
